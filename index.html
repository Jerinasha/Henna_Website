Jerin Asha | Owner & Lead Artist

The art of mehendi has been close to my heart ever since I was a child. I still remember holding a henna cone for the first time — what began as innocent doodles slowly grew into a lifelong passion. Over the years, this simple fascination turned into an artistic journey filled with creativity, patience, and love for detail.

As I started creating designs for friends and family during weddings and festivals, I discovered how each stroke of mehendi could tell a story — a story of beauty, emotion, and culture. That realization inspired me to turn my passion into something more meaningful, and that’s how “Jerin’s Mehendi Art” was born.

Today, I specialize in both traditional and contemporary styles, blending classic elegance with modern artistry. Every design I create is crafted with precision and heart — whether it’s for a bride on her big day or for someone celebrating a special occasion.

For me, mehendi is not just a form of decoration — it’s a language of expression. My mission is to bring joy and confidence through every design, turning your special moments into beautiful memories that last beyond the stain.

— JERIN ASHA

About Henna

Henna — also called mehndi — is made from the dried leaves of Lawsonia inermis and has been used for centuries to beautify and celebrate. At Jerin’s Mehendi Art we blend traditional motifs with contemporary compositions to create designs that are both culturally rooted and fashion-forward. Our paste is prepared from 100% natural henna, chosen for its dyeing and conditioning properties so the stain develops rich, lasting color while remaining gentle on skin. Whether for bridal rituals, special events, or modern body art, our work honors tradition while embracing today’s global trends.

  • 100% natural, skin-safe henna paste prepared fresh
  • Traditional and contemporary designs tailored to the occasion
  • Placement and sizing for hands, arms, feet, back and bespoke requests
